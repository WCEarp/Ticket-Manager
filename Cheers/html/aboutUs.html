About Us

For our project, we have been tasked by organizations that manage shows at the Civic Center Concert Hall and Civic Center Playhouse to create a ticket management software that they can use to run events as well as an interface that customers may use to buy tickets to events. The goals of this software given by the organizations is for it to be easily used by volunteers, easily used by multiple groups, and allow for easy manipulation of seating and seating policies. The system will also be used by customers and therefore will need an interface for customers to buy tickets. Another feature that the software will have to manage are season ticket holders as well as a special way of reserving tickets in a tiered customer system. At the end of this project, we will have created software that will be used by multiple organizations to manage their events as well as support the users and customers that will buy tickets to their shows. The software will feature a pick your seat on the map functionality and will store all appropriate data that is needed.
