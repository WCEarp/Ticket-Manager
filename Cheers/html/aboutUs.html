About Us

Welcome to Cheers!

The purpose of this website is to simulate the ticket management and ticket purchasing process used by employees of the VBC theatre and their customers.

Tickets may be purchased by visiting the "Buy Tickets" tab located at the top of any web page. Helpful tips are located on the right side of the ticket purchasing tool to guide you through the purchasing of your ticket.

Once logged in, employees may used the "Manage" tab located at the top of any web page to manage tickets and shows. Helpful tips are located on the right side of the management tools window to guide you through the process of using each featured tool.
